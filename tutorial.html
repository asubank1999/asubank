Welcome to our Upload/Search Service Tutorial

Here's a pro tip to help you search and index new subjects efficiently. Follow one of the three methods below for the best experience.

📋 The Three Methods
⏳
Method 1 — Patient Load

Click, Wait, Then Scroll

When you click on the link, please wait up to three minutes for the page to fully load. After it loads, type your subject in the search bar — but don't hit the search button yet. Instead, scroll down the page to explore the content that appears.

⚡
Method 2 — Speed Boost

Search, Then Reload

If you want to speed up your search, here's what to do: once you click the link, type your subject, hit Search, then reload the website. This way, the website refreshes with your current search, making your results faster and more accurate.

🎯
Method 3 — Best Practice

Combine Both for Best Results

For the most thorough experience, use Method 1 first to let the page fully index your subject, then apply Method 2 on your next visit. Over time your searches will become significantly faster as the content is already indexed in the system.
