Understanding Hall Numbers

Hall numbers follow a specific format to help you locate them easily. For example:
1206 means:

  • 1 = College Number (College of IT)
  • 2 = Floor Number (2nd Floor)
  • 06 = Hall Number (Hall 06)
Use this format to navigate to your desired hall.
