Classmate Matching Service

Find classmates with similar schedules and interests

How It Works

Fill out your profile with your academic interests, class schedule, and hobbies. Our system will match you with classmates who share at least 50% compatibility.

Important: Please Be Patient

After submitting your profile, you'll need to wait until another student with similar interests also submits their profile. This process may take some time depending on how many students are using the service.

Your Privacy Matters

Your personal information stays private until a match is found. Only when two students have at least 50% compatibility will contact information be exchanged via email.

Email Verification Required

To benefit from this service, you must have a working email address. Once a match is found (50%+ compatibility), both you and your match will receive an email with further instructions and contact information.
